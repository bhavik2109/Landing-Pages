# Landing Page Generation Prompt: The Genius Wave

**Role:** You are an elite Full-Stack Web Developer and Conversion-Focused UI/UX Designer.
**Objective:** Create a stunning, high-converting, and premium single-page landing page for a revolutionary neuro-audio product called **"The Genius Wave."**

---

### **1. Core Concept & Aesthetic**
- **Theme:** "Neuro-Premium" / "Scientific Elegance."
- **Color Palette:** Deep Space Black (#050505) as the primary background. Use gradients of Royal Purple (#6A0DAD), Electric Blue (#00D2FF), and Deep Gold (#D4AF37) for accents, glows, and interactive elements.
- **Visual Style:** 
    - **Glassmorphism:** Use frosted glass effects for content cards.
    - **Glow & Depth:** Subtle neon glows behind key sections to represent "brain waves" or "energy."
    - **Typography:** Modern Sans-Serif (e.g., 'Inter' or 'Outfit'). Use large, bold headings with gradient text.
    - **Animations:** Smooth scroll reveals, hover-state scaling, and pulsing glow effects on Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons.

---

### **2. Page Structure & Copy Requirements**

#### **Section 1: Hero (The Hook)**
- **Headline:** "Unlock Your Brain's Full Potential in Just 12 Minutes." (Use a premium gradient for 'Full Potential').
- **Sub-headline:** "Experience the science-backed power of Theta waves. Boost creativity, sharpen focus, and reclaim your mental clarity with just one audio session a day."
- **Primary CTA:** A large, glowing "Activate Your Genius Wave Now" button.
- **Imagery:** A high-quality, abstract visualization of a brain glowing with energy or a person in a state of zen/focus (Use a placeholder or describe a futuristic 3D render).

#### **Section 2: The Science (How It Works)**
- **Title:** "The Power of Theta Waves."
- **Content:** Briefly explain the neuroscience. "Our brain naturally produces Theta waves during deep creativity and flow. The Genius Wave uses advanced sound frequencies to guide your mind into this 'Genius State' effortlessly."
- **Visual:** Use a simple wave animation or an infographic style layout.

#### **Section 3: Key Features & Benefits (The Proof)**
- **Format:** A 3-column grid with glassmorphic cards and custom icons.
- **Card 1: 12-Minute Routine.** Focus on the speed and efficiency.
- **Card 2: Mental Clarity.** Benefits like "Goodbye, Brain Fog."
- **Card 3: Lifetime Ownership.** One-time payment, permanent access.
- **Card 4: Science-Backed.** Created by neuroscientists and sound engineers.

#### **Section 4: Pricing & Offer (The Close)**
- **Headline:** "Invest in Your Mind today."
- **Price Display:** A clean, bold "$39" price point with a "One-Time Payment" label.
- **Value Proposition:** Include the "90-Day Money-Back Guarantee" badge.
- **Secondary CTA:** "Secure Your Copy Now."

#### **Section 5: Footer**
- Links to Disclaimer, Privacy, and Terms.
- A brief scientific disclaimer about FDA evaluation.

---

### **3. Technical Implementation Details**
- **Framework:** Use Vanilla HTML5, modern CSS3 (Flexbox/Grid), and minimal JavaScript for animations.
- **Responsiveness:** Must be fully mobile-responsive (mobile-first design).
- **SEO:** Optimized meta tags, semantic headings (H1-H5), and fast loading assets.
- **Interactivity:** Buttons should have a "pulse" effect. Navigation should be sticky with a blur background.

---

**Output Style:** Provide the complete code (HTML and CSS) in a single integrated file or separate files as appropriate, ensuring the design looks "Apple-tier" in quality.
