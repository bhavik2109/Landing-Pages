Why it works

Your Brain Runs on Frequencies.
This Helps It Tune In.

Theta and Gamma brainwave states are associated with peak mental performance. The challenge is most of us never get there without years of meditation.

Brainwave entrainment is the process of using sound to gently guide your brain toward a specific frequency. When you hear slightly different tones in each ear, your brain naturally produces a third tone — a "binaural beat" — that nudges your neural activity toward that target state.

This isn't new-age speculation. The phenomenon has been documented since the 1970s. What The Genius Song does is layer this entrainment into a carefully composed audio track that's pleasant to listen to — not just a clinical tone generator.
